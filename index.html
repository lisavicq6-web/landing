Our story

Born from a day at the beach.

Our mission

Seava was born from a simple frustration: why should you have to hide your phone in your shoe or carry a bag to the waves? We believe the beach should be a place of total freedom.

We design and deploy smart locker stations directly on beaches, so that swimmers, families, and tourists can enjoy the sea without a single worry about their valuables.

Our vision

We are building the future of beach infrastructure — sustainable, intelligent, and beautifully designed. Seava stations blend into the landscape while delivering a premium, connected experience.

Security first

Frequently Asked Questions

Simply find the nearest Seava station on our map, choose your locker size, pay online, and receive your unlock code by SMS or on screen. Tap the code on the station to open your locker. When you're done, lock it and your session ends automatically.

You'll receive an SMS notification 15 minutes before your session ends. You can extend directly from the link in the SMS. Your locker will remain locked until you come back, even after expiry — we won't open it.
